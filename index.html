Mystics Revealed

Mystics Revealed is here to unveil the usages and effects of rare enchanted objects collected by John Muffins, a renowned wizard, known for casting charms on inanimate objects to give them different purposes, in the hopes to educate fellow witches, wizards and the believers in magic about these objects.

Enchanted Objects

Ring of the Phoenix

The ring of the Phoenix is made using lapis lazuli gemstone and enchanted with phoenix ashes. Just as phoenix’s rise from the dead, the ring provides one with the gift of immortality by resurrecting them on death by any cause. The ring bearer must be wearing the ring on their finger for the ring’s enchantment to work.

Disguise Mist

This mist of disguise conceals your identity from others by changing your physical appearance in the eyes of a beholder.

Talisman

This talisman is for witches and wizards who practice ancestral magic. It connects its bearer to the spiritual world from where one can channel the magic of their consecrated ancestors, giving one the power, knowledge and strength to conduct complex spells on their own.

Expunging Tape

This tape makes any inanimate object outlined by it to disappear no matter the density or thickness of the objects.

Unholy Elixir

Brewed with Unicorn blood and drops of flames from Tartarus, this unholy mixture of good and bad, makes this potion extremely dangerous to any living thing. Consuming a drop of this potion would weaken one’s soul, consuming any amount more than a drop will lead to immediate death.

Tranquil Candle

This candle is enchanted with Salvia officinalis commonly known as Sage. Lighting the candle will block out noise from your surroundings and prevents others from eavesdropping to your conversions or actions, providing you with a private space to practice your spells away from distractions.

Binding Doll

This doll is enchanted to bind itself to a human being. Once bound, the doll gives the bearer the power to control the being. For the enchantment to activate, one must hold the doll, a strand of hair of the person you want to bind it with and repeat the name of the human being three times.

Pen of Creation

This pen is enchanted to bring inanimate objects drawn using it to life. This pen can only make inanimate objects to appear when an image of the object is drawn or writing the name of the object.

About the Collector

John Muffins, the owner of this collection has been collecting rare enchanted items from a very young age. He grew his interest in enchantment objects as he became an avid charms caster.
